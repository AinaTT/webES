Renovate your house without building work and in a short time it is possible

It's been a few years since you bought your house with all the excitement in the world. However, the passage of time has taken its toll and just as you have grown older, your house has also aged.

You would like to change floors, walls, ceilings and furniture that have become obsolete and outdated. But getting into a building site and all that it implies (budget, time and rubble) makes it very difficult for you to make the move, doesn't it?

Renovation without building work and in a short time: microcement

We have the solution! Microcement is the decorative coating you are looking for. Installing microcement is easy and simple, you will invest much less money and best of all: no building work or rubble, so you will renovate your home in no time.

Furthermore, it is a material that is compatible with any type of surface (floor, wall, ceiling and furniture), both for interior and exterior rooms. In this way, the application of microcement is done directly on the existing material. Yes, as you read. You can apply microcement on tiles in the bathroom or kitchen, among others. Isn't that great?

High decorative value of microcement

Microcement offers a wide range of decorative possibilities and unique designs to let your imagination run wild. You can create microcement bathrooms, microcement kitchens, microcement stairs, microcement swimming pools and even install underfloor heating as they are totally compatible.

But the installation of microcement offers many more advantages (see article 8 reasons) such as its great mechanical resistance and resistance to abrasion; or that its cleaning and maintenance are very simple, with water and PH neutral soap is more than enough, for example.
