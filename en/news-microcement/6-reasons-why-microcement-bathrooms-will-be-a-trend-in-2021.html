6 reasons why microcement bathrooms will be a trend in 2021

If you are thinking of renovating or enlarging your bathroom in the coming months this article is for you. The microcement bathrooms, decorative coating of high performance, will be a trend in 2021. In Topciment, microcement supplier, we know very well what we are talking about, there is a reason why we are leaders in the manufacture of microcement.

Here are six reasons why microcement baths are going to be a real sensation next year. Take notes!

1. Greater sense of spaciousness

In most cases the bathroom is the smallest area of the house and therefore optimizing its size as much as possible is a priority when deciding to renovate it. The continuity of the microcement gives a greater sense of spaciousness that will be further enhanced if combined with light colours on floors and walls.

The continuity of the microcement gives a greater sense of spaciousness that will be further enhanced if combined with light colours on floors and walls.

3.Versatility

Micocement is compatible with practically all existing surfaces (walls, floors and ceilings) as well as being suitable for use in furniture. This versatility means that you can create an infinite number of designs in any colour you like, as microcement comes in a wide range of tones.

4. Simple and easy cleaning

Decorating a bathroom is one of the laziest things in the world, isn't it? A problem that with this continuous coating disappears. A microcement bath is very easy to clean because the absence of joints in this coating prevents the accumulation of dirt. All you have to do is use a neutral cleaner and a cleaning cloth for daily care and... you're done!

5.Enhances natural light

If there is something that occupies and worries when it comes to renovating a bathroom, it is that it is luminous, creating a relaxed atmosphere. Microcement is ideal because it enhances natural light as if it were reflected on it, as long as the colours used are light.

6. Quick installation

As the application of microcement does not require any building work, installation will be very quick. Just a few hours after placing the material on the surface or furniture of your choice, you will be able to walk through your new bathroom without any problems. Isn't that great?
