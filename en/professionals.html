What do we offer to professionals?

At Topciment we have been at the side of professionals in the construction sector for almost two decades. Attentive to their needs, we have evolved to offer them products and services with high added value.

In our commitment to the professionals of microcement, we not only manufacture for them and their customers the best possible decorative coatings. We offer them a range of services to help them prosper in the professional environment.

Specialized technical service

Available in several languages, our sales technicians advise and resolve any doubts the professional may have about Topciment products or about the application, care and maintenance of microcement.

Contact a technician

Microcement training courses

In order to guarantee the best possible result in the application of our continuous coatings, we provide training courses to professionals for each level. In the Topciment courses, instructors teach the application of microcement, varnishes, metallic paints or advanced techniques and textures, among others.

See types of courses

Microcement distributors

Topciment products are sold in over 58 countries through our microcement distributors. A continuously expanding network of professionals offering countless advantages.

Microcement material calculator
