FIBERGLASS MESH BUILTEX

The flexible fibreglass mesh for microcement is ideal for the reinforcement of continuous microcement surfaces and coatings because it makes the substrate more solid and allows the surface to be waterproofed.

This flexible flat fiberglass mesh is used to reinforce the edges of walls and stairs. Moreover, it prevents the appearance of cracks at the meeting of two materials of different nature and in microcement coatings on cement mortars.

Technical characteristics

Dimensions

Roll of 50 x 1m

Thickness

Builtex GR 50
Builtex GR 58
Builtex GR 160

Packaging

The number of units varies according to the weight of the mesh.

SHORT-HAIR ROLLER

Special roller for priming and sealing microcement. The WT polyester roller is particularly suitable for our water-based primers and sealers. It is a roller that provides a fine, uniform and high quality finish.

The DSV enamel roller is a product with a very short pile, which is highly recommended for our sealers, due to its resistance to solvents and acetones.

Technical characteristics

Dimensions

4 x 180 mm
4 x 220 mm

Material

Polyester fibre (WT)
Woven virgin wool (DSV)

Packaging

They are sold in unitary blister packs.
