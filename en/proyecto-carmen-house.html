Carmen House

Denia, Spain.
Photos: Mariela Apollonio.

Located in an incomparable setting in the heart of Les Marines, Denia, Carmen House has become in one of the most viral works in social networks and a true example of elegance, sophistication and continuity. Its architect, Carles Faus, has created a work in which the colour white is the authentic It acts as a generator and conductor of light.

The traceability and linearity created with the white microcement of continuity, unites the pavements The vertical and horizontal surfaces give the whole house its spaciousness.

Topciment products used:

-Sttandard Microdeck in white colour and satin finish: for floors and interior wall coverings.
- Sttandard Microbase white colour and satin finish: for outdoor flooring.
