What is Arcocem?

Arcocem is the Topciment® pigment line that can be applied to both walls and floors.

Within this line we can differentiate the six basic colors of Arcocem Basic , which are the basis of the wide spectrum of colours that make up Arcocem Plus, and which give the microcement a wide range of colours of great importance for the final result of the application.

In definitive, a wide range of pigments and textures of newest technology characterized by the high quality of its components and their optimal end result.

Arcocem Basic

Arcocem Basic is a water based pigment range used for the preparation of mono doses to colour the microcement.

Technical features

Alkali resistant

Arcocem Basic is strong to alkalis like ammonia

High colour stability

The colors of Arcocem Basic are maintained over time without alterations produced by the light or aging

Compatible with the systems watery

Arcocem Basic is compatible with the water-based paints.

Arcocem Plus

Arcocem Plus is a concentrated liquid dye for the pigmentation of microcement Topciment

Arcocem Plus is suitable for the products of the Sttandard line: Microbase, Microdeck, Microfino and Microstone.

Technical features

High colour stability

The colors of Arcocem Plus are maintained over time without alterations caused by light or aging.

Non flammable

Arcocem Plus is a dye water-based liquid.

No plasticizers, solvents, ammonia and free of emulsifiers.
Easily miscible.

Pour the dye into the resin and stir slowly with a spatula or a mechanical shaker at low revolutions.

Arcocem Oxide

Arcocem Oxide is a water-based metallic paint with fine particles of bronze whose application provides a natural bronze effect. Always check the product on a small area before proceeding the application.

Technical features

Applicable to any type of support.

Microcement,concrete,brick,ceramic,panel drywall, plaster, glass, paper and metals.

High adherence to the support

In low absorption areas it is recommended that the use of our adhesion promoter Primacem Plus.

Prodotto pronto all'uso

Non può essere diluito con acqua.

Versatilità di applicazione

Can be applied with a trowel, Brush, brush, sponge, roller or gun.

Arcocem Metal

Arcocem Metal is a range of pigments in aqueous dispersion for application of glazes with metallic reflections in different finishes.

Designed for use as decorative paints. Always check the product on a small area before application.

Technical features

Applicable over any type of support.

Microcement,concrete,brick,ceramic,panel drywall, plaster, glass, paper and metals.

Versatilità di applicazione

Can be applied with a trowel, brush or paintbrush.

Prodotto pronto all'uso

Non può essere diluito con acqua.

Frequent questions

¿Qué pasa si hay juntas?

Es necesario nivelar la superficie con Efectto Super Grain si hay juntas. Si las juntas son demasiado anchas se tendrá que utilizar un mortero de reparación de baja retracción.
